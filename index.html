What is bst.coach?

We are a comprehensive online-based strength & conditioning program primarily made for youth athletesâ€™ (ages 12-18) general athleticism development. When completed, the program will offer a rich online based exercise video database in a form of well-designed training routines and their progressions. Our program will cover all basic aspects of general athleticism that a youth athlete of most sports need to have as their foundations. BST stands for: Beyond Skills Training.

What are the two main goals of BST?

The two primary goals of this program are:

  • To build healthier and more athletic youth sports athletes.
  • To reduce the load for coaches by providing them with good and affordable general athleticism training resources.

When will we launch?
